PACKAGER

To help you make the most effective use of your materials!

How it works:

  1. If you have a certain amount of cardboard, plastic, paper, wood, concrete, etc and you need to find how to make a box or silo that will hold the most amount of material, this is the helper for you
  2. First, you enter the amount of material you have in terms of area,(i.e. cm squared, ft squared) do not enter units
  3. Then select the shape you would like to form(A box or a cylinder)
  4. Then simply click calculate and the program will calculate the measurements that will give you the greatest amount of space with the amount of material you have
  5. This uses optimization methods from Calculus
